How does it work?

Our team searches the web all day long looking for mistake fares and rediculously cheap flights. As soon as we find one we email our list immediately to notify you of this flight, giving you a chance to book it before it's gone.

What is a mistake fare?

Airlines make mistakes all the time and accidentally undercharge for flights to some of the most incredible locations around the globe such as Costa Rica, France, Bali, Iceland, etc. & Domestic locations as well

Is there a free plan?

What's the difference between Premium vs Free?

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You may cancel your membership anytime, no questions asked and hassle free. This can be done anytime by logging into your account.

How do I know I'll get good deals?

We never take a commission and we are entirely supported by our paid members which means our only incentive is to find you the best and cheapest deals out there.

How many deals should I expect?

The number of deals you receive depends on how many deals come up in a given week from the airports you select. We can’t control where deals originate. We search for deals from all airports, large and small. If you live close to a smaller or expensive airport, there may be fewer deals that pop up. We recommend that you select all international airports within a few hours of your home airport to maximize the number of deals that you receive.
